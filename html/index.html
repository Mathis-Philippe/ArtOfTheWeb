About this site

The Art of Web is a showcase and testing ground for Chirp Internet, a web development and consulting company that has been building interactive websites since 1997. Chirp was originally based in Canberra, Australia, but in 2003 we re-located to the Netherlands, and then in 2006 to Sibiu, Romania. Our clients are mostly in Australia, Europe and the United States.

If you have problems implementing any of the code presented on this site, or are looking for something we haven't covered, then you can contact us through the feedback link at the top of the page. We welcome comments, corrections, questions and criticism.
